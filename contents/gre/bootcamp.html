The Math Boot Camp is a workshop program offered by Georgia Tech School of Mathematics to prepare undergraduate students for the GRE Math Subject Exam. These workshops are available free of charge to all undergraduate students in the Atlanta area and students visiting Georgia Tech as part of an REU program. No registration is necessary.
